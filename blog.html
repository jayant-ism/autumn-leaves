Affiliate Marketing

Affiliate marketing is the process of earning a commission by promoting other people’s (or company’s) products. You find a product you like, promote it to others, and earn a piece of the profit for each sale that you make.

Business to Business

BUSINESS TO BUSINESS MARKETING” (B2B MARKETING) refers to the exchange of either goods or services or both between two businesses where it involves the sale of goods and services by one organization to the other which either sells them to the consumers or use them to support their system

Marketing through newsletters and articles

Newsletter Marketing involves sending out an informational newsletter to interested parties through emails. Article Marketing is a branch of content marketing which is a type of advertising where companies write and distribute short articles to a range of outlets.

Internal Marketing

Internal Marketing is promoting the company’s vision, goals, culture, and mission statements within the internal customers (the employees) by creating an emotional connection to the brand.

Social Media Marketing

Social media marketing (or S.M.M.), is a form of internet marketing that involves creating and sharing content on social media networks in order to achieve your marketing and branding goals.

Inbound Marketing

Inbound Marketing means doing business humanly, creating a meaningful relationship with visitors in the prospect of customers.
